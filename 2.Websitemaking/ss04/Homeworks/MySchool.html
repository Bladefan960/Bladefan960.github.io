My School

I attend Rancho San Joaquin Middle School.

Info: Rancho San Joaquin school is an intermediate middle school located at 4861 Michelson Dr, Irvine, CA 92612. Founded originally to be a high school, rancho is spacious and provides many activities for students to enjoy. At rancho, academic competition is high, with many talented students in areas like math, science, sports, music, video editing, writing, and photoshop. Rancho focuses on safety through the RULES system, excellence in academics, growth in academics, and enjoyment through unique activities in school. Overall Rancho is a wonderful school that everyone should join!
